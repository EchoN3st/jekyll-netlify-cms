About

Veilon.org is a space for thought.

Written by EchoNest, a cybersecurity professional, this site explores technical ideas or otherwise, that shape how we understand the internet, security, and our place within it.

Some posts are detailed. Some are simple. Some revisit known ground with new eyes. The goal isn't to break news or build hype. Just to look closely, think clearly, and question what we take for granted.

There's no schedule. Posts appear when there's something worth saying.

If you're curious, you're welcome.
